PREVENT YOURSELF FROM COVID-19

Wash your hands frequently

Wash hands before eating, after sneezing, or coughing, and after coming in contact with someone who shows symptoms.

Carry a Sanitizer

Cleaning your hands using sanitizer is an effective and practical way to keep your hands clean in public places without getting your hands wet.

Use a facemask

A face mask gives you basic protection against airborne germs and infections, especially at crowded places.

Maintain social distancing

If someone around you is coughing or sneezing, try to maintain a distance of 1 meter and cover your mouth and eyes.

Seek medical care early

If you or someone you know is showing symptoms like cough, fever, or difficulty breathing, visit a doctor as soon as possible.

Stay informed and updated

Keep a check on Coronavirus updates in your locality. Avoid parts of the city that have confirmed cases of the disease.

Disinfect your home

Wash your bedsheets and towels. Wipe down counters, tabletops, doorknobs, bathroom fixtures, toilets, phones, keyboards, tablets, and bedside tables every 2 days.

Stay home stay safe

Try to spend most of the time with your family at your home. Avoid going public places.
